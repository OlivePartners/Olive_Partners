ABOUT OLIVE PARTNERS

A Disciplined, Long-Term Investment Approach

Olive Partners Limited is a privately owned UK property investment company focused on the acquisition, management, and long-term stewardship of residential real estate assets. The company operates with a disciplined approach, prioritising capital preservation, income stability, and sustainable long-term value creation.

We focus on assets supported by strong underlying fundamentals, including location quality, tenant demand, and long-term resilience. Rather than pursuing short-term gains, our strategy is centred on long-term ownership and operational excellence.

Working alongside trusted agents, advisers, and operating partners, we ensure assets are managed efficiently, responsibly, and in accordance with applicable standards.

INVESTMENT PHILOSOPHY

Assisted Living Sector

Olive Partners Limited is actively developing its presence in the assisted living sector, recognising the growing demand for high-quality supported housing across the UK. We partner with specialist operators to deliver accommodation that supports residents with dignity and care.
