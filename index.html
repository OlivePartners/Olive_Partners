About Olive Partners

Olive Partners Ltd is a UK property company focused on the creation and stewardship of high-quality residential assets in markets with enduring demand. We adopt a disciplined, selective approach to acquisition and refurbishment, underpinned by strong fundamentals and long-term thinking, to deliver resilient homes characterised by quality, longevity, and consistency.

Assisted Living

Olive Partners Ltd develops high-quality residential properties purpose-designed for supported and assisted living. We work closely with care providers to deliver safe, compliant, and adaptable homes that meet real local needs—supporting long-term tenancy, operational stability, and sustainable outcomes for residents and operators.
