About Olive Partners

Olive Partners Ltd is a UK property investment company focused on delivering high-quality homes where demand is strongest. We invest with discipline, refurbish with purpose, and partner for the long term—creating well-run residential assets.

Our Investment Philosophy

We apply a rigorous, research-driven framework to every acquisition and management decision.

Quality Asset Acquisition

We target well-located residential properties with strong fundamentals, sustainable demand, and long-term capital preservation characteristics.

Professional Asset Management

Every property in our portfolio is managed to the highest professional standards, ensuring compliance, tenant welfare, and asset integrity.

Structured Partnerships

We work with trusted agents, advisers, and operating partners through clear, transparent structures that align incentives and deliver results.

Assisted Living Sector

Olive Partners Ltd develops high-quality residential properties purpose-designed for supported and assisted living. We work closely with care providers to deliver safe, compliant, and adaptable homes that meet real local needs.
